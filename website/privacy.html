Privacy Policy

This privacy policy has been created to let the users of our website know what their personal infomation is being used for online. Check below to gather a clear understanding of how we collect and use your information for our website.

WHEN DO WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?


When signing up for our site, we will collect details such as your name, and email address to create a better experience for you.
If you also decide to use other features such as our quiz, your age, BMI, and other facets of your health may be collected to examine your risk of having a stroke.


COOKIES

Most of your information will be stored in a small text file know as a cookie, allowing us to keep track of your settings, preferences and other information that will create a better experience whilst using the site.
You can choose to enable or disable cookies on your browser, however if they are disabled some features may not function properly.


WHAT IS YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION USED FOR

The information that we collect will not be sold or given to any third parties. It is used for the sole purpose of creating a better experience for the user on our website, by allowing them to use features that could not be used without it.


THIRD PARTIES

All information collected will not be sold or transfered to third parties
On our site we include links to external websites, where your information may be collected by them. To find out more check the external websites privacy policy. We are not responsible for any of your personal information being tracked by external sites.
