Privacy Policy

Here at strokehelp.org we use personal information collected to provide the features and functionality of the site:

- Collected information includes such personal details as weight, height, body mass index, email addresses and passwords.
- This data is used to both identify the user and their details as well as track health progress and provide personalised information and services.
- Data collected is kept privately on our servers and will never be shared with a third party.
- Please contact us about any privacy concerns.
