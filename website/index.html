ABOUT US

StrokeHelp.com was created and designed to provide support and knowledge to those whom desire the latest support from the most trusted sources. Our site and services were developed in order to raise the right aweness to help anybody identify and diagnose the effects of stroke early in order to help save people’s lives. The fundamental aim of our service is to alert the general public on how to save the lives of stroke victims and to support them after the stroke to get them back on their feet.


STROKES

A stroke is a deadly "brain attack" that occurs due to the lack of blood flow to the brain. This is usually due to an artery bursting or from the artery becoming blocked. Click here to learn more.

STROKE TREATMENT

There are various treatments available to stroke patients ranging from preventative treatments to post stroke treatments. Click here to learn more.

INTEGRATIVE MEDICINE

Integrative medicine is a philosophy of healthcare, where Western medicines are combined with alternate treatments in order to treat ailments. Click here to learn more.
